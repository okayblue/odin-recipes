Brownies

Description

Delicious and easy to make brownies.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F, grease and flour 8-inch square pan.
  2. Stir butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  3. Beat in cocoa, flour, salt, and baking powder.
  4. Spread into prepared pan. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes.
